Chocolate Pizza

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissvolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until chuncky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completly.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granualated sugar until sugar is completly dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Wisk in the egg yokes. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to the manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tuperware. Freeze at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1 lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yokes
  • 1 lemon juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans,roughly chopped
